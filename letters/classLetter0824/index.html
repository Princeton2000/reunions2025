Paper Letter…in the year 2024?

Wednesday, August 21, 2024 at 1:02:00 PM

Aug 21, 2024

Dear Classmate,

Yes!

Sorry, I just wanted to try that out…what a thrill! Hope you felt it too. Really, this letter is meant to catch you up on what’s going on with your Class of 2000 as we gear up for our 25th Reunion this May 22-25th. Our email system has been unreliable, so we’re going analog to make sure you have the most important information and can stay in touch through the year.

There are three things to remember:- reunions.princeton2000.org- Registration opens November 1- Please join our whatsapp broadcast channel here: https://bit.ly/P2000Announcements

Reunions Site

The most important thing to remember is reunions.princeton2000.org. That will be the source of truth for all reunions information going forward. All information will be posted there first. The site is live now and has information on housing, schedule, kids activities, and frequently asked questions. Some information, like the schedule, is still only a skeleton, so check for updates throughout the year.

Registration

Our 25th Reunion will be May 22-25, 2025. That is the most important date on your calendar going forward. Just make sure you’re in Princeton those days, and everything else will pretty much work out for you. That said, the second-most important date is Nov 1, 2024, when we open registration. If you’re looking for a slightly painful point of reference, yes, that is actually 29 years to the day from our Early Action application deadline. Woof.

Anyway, you can go to reunions.princeton2000.org on November 1 and we will have an incredibly prominent link to registration right at the top. We’d really appreciate it if you can register early, and are offering significant discounts for those that do. The more people we know are coming, the more budget we can commit to our food, beverage, and entertainment, the less waste we’ll have for our order quantities, and early cash flow helps a ton with our vendor deposits. If you know you’re coming, and you know you will, register early! And even if you can’t make Reunions, you can still reserve your jacket. November 1!

Communications

The University has made some unfortunate and significant changes to Tigernet email over the last couple of years. It seems that only about 60% of you are receiving our emails, and it’s a different 60% each time. If you haven’t received our emails, I’m really sorry about that. We’re fixing it in a couple of ways.

First, if you set up Tigernet forwarding to your personal email through an @alumni.princeton.edu account, we know that’s broken and responsible for many lost emails. Please go to tigernet.princeton.edu and update your email address to just your primary account.

Second, if you know you’re not getting emails from Tigernet, I’m happy to email you directly, just drop me an email at jpurnell@alumni. Also, please check in with your friends if you get an email from us and see if they got it. It really goes a long way.

Third, we’ve set up a channel on WhatsApp that will be used for official class announcements. We all remember the great reply-all debacle of 1998, so I promise it’s not an 1,100-person group chat, just key information on key dates. As a side benefit, it will also give us a way to reach everyone during Reunions if we need to adjust anything on the fly. We really encourage you to join the channel via the QR code or at https://bit.ly/P2000Announcements. We’ll also send out announcements through the following:

  • Facebook: https://bit.ly/P2000FacebookGroup
  • Instagram: https://bit.ly/P2000Instagram

OK. So to recap.

  • Right now: https://bit.ly/P2000Announcements.
  • November 1: Registration: reunions.princeton2000.org.
  • Anytime: reunions.princeton2000.org.
  • May 22-25: Reunions at Princeton with 2000. Org.
